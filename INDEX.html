Iron Rules

Hit your protein target above everything else. Muscle won't come without it.
Eat your biggest carb meals around your workout — before and after.
Don't skip meals. Being skinny means your body burns through calories fast.
Creatine monohydrate 3–5g/day is the one supplement worth it — cheap and proven.

Recovery & Secrets

The hidden 50% of your progress happens outside the gym.

Recovery Essentials

Sleep 7.5–8 hours every night. This is non-negotiable — 90% of muscle is built during deep sleep.
Don't train the same muscle group two days in a row. The split is already designed around this.
Stay hydrated — 3–4L water/day. Muscles are ~75% water; dehydration tanks performance.
Stretch chest and shoulders post-workout. Tight pecs ruin posture and shrink your visual presence.

Training Technique

Focus on mind-muscle connection, not just moving weight. Squeeze at the top of every rep.
Progressive overload: add 1 rep or 1–2.5kg every week. Write your lifts down each session.
Rest 90–120 sec between sets for hypertrophy. Don't rush it.
For lateral raises especially: lighter weight, strict form. This is what builds the wide-shoulder look.

The Aesthetic Cheatcode

Build the V-Taper: Your V-taper (broad shoulders + narrow waist) is already partly built in at your stats. Shoulders + lats are the priority lifts — OHP, lateral raises, pull-ups, lat pulldowns.
Instant Width: Stand tall. Posture adds 3–4cm to perceived shoulder width instantly. Chest up, shoulder blades back.
